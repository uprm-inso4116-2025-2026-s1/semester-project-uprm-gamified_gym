____
Guide questions for domain modeling:
____

[arabic]
. Are there missing domain concepts that people expect in a
gym/gamification app?
. Are relationships between entities explicit and natural?
. Does terminology match how fitness users describe concepts?
. Can new features (leaderboards, rewards, challenges, streaks) be added
without structural refactoring?
. Are lifecycle rules (session completion, reward redemption) clear?
. Are expected domain concepts present (User, Membership, Workout,
Exercise, Plan, Challenge, Reward, Leaderboard)?
. Are any real-world concepts missing or overly simplified?
. Would a non-developer domain expert understand the model without
translation?
. Is sensitive data separated and access-controlled in the model?
. Can typical flows be unit-tested?
. Is temporal data (workout history, progress over time) explicitly to
support analytics?
