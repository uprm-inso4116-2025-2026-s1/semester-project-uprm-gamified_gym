*[.underline]#Scenarios:#*

[arabic]
. Ivania signs up for the app using her student email. During
onboarding, she selects “strength training” as her goal and sets her
fitness level to intermediate. The app generates a structured weekly
plan.

____
On her first day, she logs a guided strength workout using the in-app
tracker. After finishing, she earns her “First Workout Logged” badge,
which gives her a motivational boost. She shares her progress on the
community feed, receiving likes and supportive comments from peers. This
reinforces her self-confidence and motivates her to keep pushing her
limits both physically and mentally.
____

[arabic, start=2]
. Samuel downloads the app skeptically, since he believes most fitness
apps are scams. When signing up, he is surprised by the clear breakdown
of features with no hidden fees.

____
He customizes his goals for body transformation and the app generates
personalized daily workouts plus a meal plan. During his workout, he
streams music directly in-app and uses the rest timer between sets.

After completing his session, he sees his streak counter increase and
gets invited to a weekly fitness challenge with friends. This
combination of personalization, transparency, and social engagement
convinces him that the app is different from the “fake” ones he used to
distrust.
____

[arabic, start=3]
. Laura signs up with her phone number and chooses the “muscle growth”
path. She schedules her weekly workouts and logs them three times a
week.

____
After each session, the app provides free nutrition ideas tied to her
workout goals, which she appreciates compared to other apps with hidden
fees.

She joins a virtual challenge group for students, where she tracks her
progress against peers. The supportive environment encourages her to
stay consistent, while the app’s simple design (similar to Strava, which
she already likes) makes logging workouts easy and enjoyable.
____

[arabic, start=4]
. Lianette signs in with her Google account. She selects “injury
recovery & strength support” as her fitness focus, since her priority is
improving knee strength for Judo.

____
The app tailors her plan with knee-strengthening exercises. While
training, she streams her favorite playlist directly from the app,
keeping her motivated.

As she stays consistent, she earns a “Recovery Warrior” badge for
completing 10 workouts focused on rehabilitation. She shares her
recovery routine with teammates, proving the app’s value for athletes
dealing with injuries.
____

[arabic, start=5]
. Aidan creates his profile and selects “overall wellness” as his goal.
The app builds a balanced routine of strength, cardio, and mindfulness
sessions.

____
He starts logging meals using the calorie tracker, which provides
nutrition guidance aligned with his fitness goals.

After maintaining his streak for a week, he earns a “Consistency Star”
badge and climbs the community leaderboard, motivating him to stay
active. The combination of progress tracking, health monitoring, and
light competition helps him maintain both his physical and mental
well-being.
____

[arabic, start=6]
. Adriana signs up with her university email and sets goals for
strength, stamina, and mental well-being. The app creates a personalized
workout plan with clear demo videos. She uses the Personal Trainer
feature for guidance during her first session and logs her workout.
Afterward, she earns a “Stamina Builder” badge and shares her progress
in the community feed, staying motivated by connecting with peers.
