=== 1. User 

* *Role:* Core entity representing the app user (gym-goer).
* *Responsibilities:* Stores personal data, progress, completed
workouts, challenges, achievements, rewards, level, and streaks.

{empty}2. Workout Session

* *Role:* Represents a single gym session logged by the User.
* *Responsibilities:* Contains exercises, tracks metrics like duration,
calories burned, and triggers achievement checks.

=== 3. Exercise 

* *Role:* Atomic unit of a Workout Session.
* *Responsibilities:* Tracks exercise-specific stats (sets, reps,
duration, calories).

=== 4. Goal 

* *Role:* Represents user-defined or system-defined targets.
* *Responsibilities:* Tracks progress (frequency, consistency,
performance).

____
Completion linked to Achievements and Rewards.
____

=== 5. Achievement 

* *Role:* Gamification reward for reaching milestones.
* *Responsibilities:* Recognizes completion of Goals, streaks, or
Challenges. May grant Rewards or badges.

=== 6. Reward 

* *Role:* Incentives to motivate Users.
* *Responsibilities:* Can be points, badges, in-app currency, or
unlockables. Linked to Achievements, Levels, or Challenge completion.

=== 7. Level 

* *Role:* Represents progression in gamification.
* *Responsibilities:* Tracks XP accumulation and unlocks higher-level
features. Each User has a Level.

=== 8. Challenge 

* *Role:* Social or individual competitions.
* *Responsibilities:* Tracks participating Users, goals, or exercises
for completion. Grants Achievements/Rewards upon success.

* New entities like *Badges*, *Community Events*, or *Friend
interactions* can be added by linking them with *User* or *Challenge*.
* Many-to-many relationships allow social and competitive features
without altering the core model.
